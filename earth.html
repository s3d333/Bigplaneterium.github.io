Earth – Our Home Planet

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. It's a beautiful blue marble floating in the vastness of space.

Key Facts

Distance from Sun: ~150 million km

Type: Terrestrial planet

Average Temperature: ~15°C / 59°F

Fun Facts

  • It's the only planet not named after a god.
  • About 71% of the surface is covered by water.
  • It's the only known planet with life.
  • Takes 365.25 days to orbit the Sun.
  • Has exactly one natural satellite — the Moon.
